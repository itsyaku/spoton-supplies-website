Our Story

From one small car to a nationwide fleet.

Our journey began in 2020, during the uncertainty of the COVID-19 pandemic. What started in a small garage quickly became a mission driven by resilience, hard work and a commitment to exceptional service.

In the early days, every delivery was personally made by our founder using his own small car — ensuring hands-on service and strong relationships with our customers from the very beginning.

From those humble beginnings, we have grown into a dedicated team of 10 employees with three delivery vehicles, proudly serving our customers with reliability and efficiency. We are driven by the belief that quality products, dependable delivery and genuine customer care make all the difference.
